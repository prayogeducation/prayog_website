We are very happy to have great relationships with other start ups and are always looking to forge new relationships. If you know a start up you think could benefit from partnering with us or who might be able to help us out then we'd love to hear from you

Piper is an Oxford-based start-up that aims to provide high-quality, affordable mini-computers to children studying in rural schools to realise their creative potential to the fullest. These mini-computers are optimised to function in rural conditions where access to electricity is unreliable and an internet connection is a luxury. Relevant education content that will supplement classroom teaching is pre-loaded on to the sturdy battery-operated computers, allowing the material to be accessed offline.
